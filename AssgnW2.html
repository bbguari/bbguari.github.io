Walmarts per million residents, based on 2010 Census data and 2013 Wal Mart store locations.

Last Friday, Walmart held its annual meeting shareholder meeting at the retailer's Arkansas headquarters. Arkansas and its southern neighbors have the most Walmarts per capita in the United States. But where are the other big Walmart states? New Hampshire, for one, where you can live free (and down the road from a Walmart) or die. With roughly 24 stores per every million citizens, New Hampshire has the 6th greatest concentration of Walmarts in the US.
